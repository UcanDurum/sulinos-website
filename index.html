About

SulinOS is an independent, fast, minimal, general purpose, free open source GNU/Linux distribution made by the community for Linux users who want to compile their apps easily.

Simplicity

Sulinos is simple because it uses Sulin software, a lightweight alternative to GNU software. Sulin is so lightweight as it is only 100mb.

Security

SulinOS is secure because every process requires authentication and wireless connections are held with SSL.

Community

SulinOS exists thanks to contributions of the its great community of people (flesh humans). Everyone can contribute to SulinOS in many different ways.
